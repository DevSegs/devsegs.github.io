AI Evaluation & Quality Benchmark Framework

A formal system for defining AI output quality standards, accuracy benchmarks, latency thresholds, and monitoring requirements — built for regulated enterprise environments where explainability and auditability are non-negotiable.

  • 01Define output quality dimensions (accuracy, latency, explainability, compliance)
  • 02Set measurable benchmarks per dimension with enterprise thresholds
  • 03Establish real-time monitoring and alert protocols
  • 04Build auditability trail for regulated environment review
◈
Segmentation-to-Expansion Opportunity Model

A data-driven approach to surface untapped product expansion within an existing customer base — built at Microsoft using EAP and GA usage data to identify $100M+ in M365 Copilot expansion opportunity.

  • 01Map product usage signals across EAP and GA cohorts
  • 02Identify engagement gaps vs. full product capability
  • 03Score accounts by expansion potential and time-to-value
  • 04Build targeted adoption playbooks for each segment tier

Regulated Product Launch Playbook

An end-to-end launch framework built for compliance-constrained environments — from requirements definition and regulatory review, through KOL activation, field toolkit build, and post-launch adoption tracking.

  • 01Define launch requirements and acceptance criteria with regulatory alignment
  • 02Build messaging architecture on clinical/technical evidence base
  • 03Activate KOLs and institutional stakeholders pre-launch
  • 04Execute phased rollout with adoption milestones and feedback loops

Voice of Customer → Roadmap Pipeline

A structured system for converting field intelligence and customer feedback into actionable roadmap input — built at Microsoft from 100+ account team engagements that directly shaped engineering priorities.

  • 01Systematically capture VoC and VoF signals across account teams
  • 02Categorize by theme, frequency, and revenue impact
  • 03Translate into product requirements and engineering briefs
  • 04Close the loop with field teams on roadmap decisions made

Developer Ecosystem Activation Model

A repeatable GTM infrastructure for activating developer communities at scale — built for the AKS Partner Forum and GitHub Copilot hackathon, reaching 635K+ developers across multiple channels.

  • 04Track trial-to-production conversion as primary success metric

Time-to-Value Benchmarking System

A measurement framework defining what "fast adoption" looks like at each stage of the product lifecycle — built for M365 Copilot and adopted by 6 internal Microsoft teams as the standard TTV reference.

  • 01Define value milestones for each user segment and use case
  • 02Set TTV benchmarks from onboarding to first meaningful outcome
  • 03Build monitoring dashboards to track cohort progression
  • 04Use TTV gaps to drive product iteration and enablement priorities
